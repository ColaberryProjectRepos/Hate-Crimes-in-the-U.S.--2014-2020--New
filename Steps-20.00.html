Create a Hate Crimes by State 20



Steps
1. Copy and paste the Hate Crimes by Year | Month chart and paste on the canvas.
2. Drag the chart to an open space on the canvas
3. In visualization, replace the X-axis with State_Name
4. Change the Chart to a Shape Map
4. In Color saturation, add the Hate Crimes measure from the Measures table
5. Add Tooltips and organize as follows

  • Hate Crimes
  • VHC Rate
  • N-VHC Rate
  • Hate Crimes YoY%
  • VHC YoY%
  • N-VHC YoY%

5. In General, format as follows

  • Properties: Height-323, Width-421
  • Position: Horizontal-859, Vertical-397
  • Title-On, Replace title with "Hate Crimes in the US", Text Color-#FFFFFF



Next, fill the map

6. In Visual, select "Fill Colors" 

  • Gradient-Off
  • Under Colors, click More and enter: enter Min- #1A5866, Max-E2713B
  • Legend-Off
  • Blank areas-On, Color-#CCCCCC
  • Border-On, Color-#CCCCCC
