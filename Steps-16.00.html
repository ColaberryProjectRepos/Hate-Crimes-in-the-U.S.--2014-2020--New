Create Hate Crimes by Year and Month Chart 16


Steps (Hate Crimes by Year & Month Chart)

  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the Visualizations pane select Line and Stacked Column Chart

Build Visual tab 
Populate the various fields as follows:

  • X-Axis: Year, Month 
  • Column values: Violent Hate Crimes (VHC) and Non-Violent Hate Crimes (N-VHC).  Rename both columns as VHC & N-VHC
  • Line values: VHC YoY%, N-VHC YoY%, Hate Crimes YoY%
  • Tool tips: VHC Rate, N-VHC Rate, Hate Crimes




Format Visual as follows:
General: 

  • Properties, Size, Height=326 Width=456, Position, Horizontal=224, Vertical=70
  • Title=On, Text=Hate Crimes by Year | Month, Font=Trebuchet MS (14), Text color=#FFFFFF, Background color=#7EB2BC, Horizontal alignment=Center
  • Effects: Background=Of, Visual boarder=On, Boarder color-#CCCCCC




Visual:

  • X-axis: Values: Font=Trebuchet MS, Size-9, Font color= #1A1A1A , Title = Off
  • Y-axis: Values: Font= Trebuchet MS, Size-9, Font color= #287E8F, Display Units=Thousands
     Title = Off
  • Secondary Y-axis: Range: Align Zeros = On
     Values: Font=Trebuchet MS, Size-9, Font color=#F8C464  Display Units=Auto
     Title = Off



Visual Cont'd

  • Gridlines: Style-Dotted, Color-#CCCCCC, Width-3px
  • Columns: VHC-##BAA55, N-VHC-#287E8F
  • Lines: Shape-Solid, Style-Solid, Width-2 px, Shade area-On
  • Colors: Hate Crime YoY%-#CB6636, N-VHC%-#54CEC8, VHC%-#F8C464
  • Markers-Off, Data Labels-On, Label Density-50%
  • Values: Font-Trebuchet MS, Size-9, Color-#1A1A1A, Display Units-Thousands
  • Background-Off
  • Series labels-Off
  • Total labels-On
