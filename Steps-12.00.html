Set Background and Add Title and Logo 12


Steps (Adding background color)

1. In Report View, go to 'Format'. Click on the Format Icon to expand the options

2. Click on 'Color' and then click on 'More Colors'. In the HEX box, type in #FAFEFF and save.

3. Set Transparency to 0%




Steps (Adding a Title)

1. On the Power Bi Ribbon, select 'Insert' then 'Text Box

2. Click on the 'Format' icon and set  follows:

       Properties: Height=70; Width=677Position: Horizontal=224; Vertical=0

       Title On; Text=Hate Crimes in the U.S. (2014-2020); Heading 3; Font=Trebuchet MS; Text Color=#333333;             Background On; Color= #FAFEFF

       Effects: Background On; Color= #FAFEFF; Transparency=0




Steps (Adding a Logo)

1. Click on Insert and then Image

2. Go to General and format as follows:

       Properties: Height=70; Width=223

       Position: Horizontal=0; Vertical=0

·                    Title=Off

·                    Effects: Visual border=On; Color=#CCCCCC; Rounded Corner=0px; Shadow=Off
