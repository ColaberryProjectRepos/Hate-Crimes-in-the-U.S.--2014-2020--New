Create a Hate Crimes by Offender Race Chart 18


Steps

1. Copy and paste the Hate Crimes by Year | Month chart and paste on an empty space on the canvas.
2. Drag the chart to an open space on the canvas
3. Replace the X-axis with Offender Race
4. In Format Visual, set as follows: 

General

  • Properties: Height-323, Width-468
  • Position: Horizontal-0, Vertical-394
  • Title-On, Title-Hate Crimes by Offender Race, Text Color-#FFFFFF

Visual

  • Data labels-On, Orientation-Vertical, Position Inside end
  • Total labels-Off
  • Sort X-axis by Hates Crimes, Descending
