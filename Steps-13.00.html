Add a Year Slicer 13


1. In Report View, go to Visualization and click on Slicer. 
2. Click on Year in the Calendar Date. 
3. Go to Format and select Visual. Format slicer visual as follows:

  • Slicer settings-Between; Slicer header-Off; 
  • Values: Font-Trebuchet MS; Size-9; Font color-#FFFFFF; Background-On; Color- #5398A5
  • Slider-On; Color-#605E5C

4. In General, format as shown below:

  • Properties: Height-88; Width-200
  • Position: Horizontal-888; Vertical-0
  • Title-Off
  • Effects: Background-Off
  • Visual Border-On; Color-#CCCCCC
